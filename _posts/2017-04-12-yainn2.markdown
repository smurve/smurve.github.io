### Neural Networks are just functions

\frac{\partial}{\partial w^l_{ij}}\sigma(\mathbf{W}^l \cdot \vec{x} + \vec{b^l}) = 
\frac{\partial }{\partial w^l_{ij}} \pmatrix{\
	\sigma(\sum_k w^l_{k1} \cdot x_k + b_1 )
	\cr \cdots \cr 
	\sigma(\sum_k w^l_{kN} \cdot x_k + b_N)}
\end{equation}
